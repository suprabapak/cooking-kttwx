Spoon Rolls

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1 pkg. dry yeast 2 c. warm water 1/2 c. melted butter 4 c. self-rising flour 1 egg, beaten 1/4 c. sugar Directions : Dissolve yeast in warm water; let stand 5 minutes. Combine yeast mixture with butter and flour in bowl; mix well. Stir in egg and sugar. Refrigerate overnight. Spoon butter into greased muffin pans; fill 2/3 full. Bake at 350u00b0 for 25 minutes.

Springerle

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 4 eggs 1 tsp. almond extract 3/4 tsp. baking soda 2 c. sugar 4 c. flour Directions : Beat eggs until light. Add sugar and beat until mixture is very stiff and light, about 15 minutes at high speed on electric mixer. Add extract, flour and soda; mix thoroughly. Roll out on lightly floured surface with regular rolling pin to 1/2 to 1/4-inch thickness. Roll with springerle rolling pin to 1/4-inch thickness, pressing only once for clear imprint.

Squash Casserole

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 2 c. cooked squash 2 eggs, well beaten 1 c. grated cheese 1 medium bell pepper 1 tsp. salt 1/2 c. melted light margarine 1 c. bread crumbs Directions : Chop bell pepper and cook in a little oil. Mix squash, eggs, cheese, bell pepper, salt, butter and 1/2 the bread crumbs. Pour into baking dish. Sprinkle with remaining crumbs. Bake 1 hour at 350u00b0 uncovered.
