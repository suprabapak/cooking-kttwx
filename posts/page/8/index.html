Asparagus Casserole

Ingredients : 3 cans asparagus spears 1 can water chestnuts 1 stick oleo 1 c. grated cheese 1 can mushroom soup 4 slices bread Directions : Melt oleo; slice bread in finger size slices. Dip in melted oleo and set aside. Drain cooked asparagus and place in a long casserole dish. Cut water chestnuts and place on top. I use the juice too. Put cheese on that, then cover with mushroom soup.
