No Peek Chicken

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1 can cream of celery soup 1 can water 1 c. regular rice 6 pieces chicken 1/2 pkg. dry onion soup mix Directions : Line bottom of 9 x 13-inch pan with foil. Sprinkle the rice on the foil and place chicken pieces on rice. Mix soup and water, then spread over chicken. Sprinkle onion soup mix over all. Cover and seal. Bake at 350u00b0 for 2 1/2 hours, without peeking.

Oatmeal Bread

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1 pkg. yeast 1/2 c. lukewarm water 2 c. uncooked oatmeal 1/2 c. brown sugar 2 Tbsp. shortening 2 tsp. salt 2 c. boiling water 4 c. flour 1/2 c. nut meats Directions : Dissolve yeast in lukewarm water. Pour boiling water over oatmeal, shortening, sugar and salt. Mix well. Let cool until lukewarm. Stir in dissolved yeast. Add nut meats and 3 1/4 cups of flour.

Oatmeal Cake
