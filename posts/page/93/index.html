Fresh Strawberry Pie

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1 baked pie shell 1 qt. cleaned strawberries 1 1/2 c. water 4 Tbsp. cornstarch 1 c. sugar 1/8 tsp. salt 4 Tbsp. strawberry jello Directions : Mix water, cornstarch, sugar and salt in saucepan. Stir constantly and boil until thick and clear. Remove from heat and stir in jello. Set aside to cool. But dont allow it to set. Layer strawberries in baked crust. Pour cooled glaze over.
