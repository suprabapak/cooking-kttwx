Fresh Broccoli Salad

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 2 bunches fresh broccoli 10 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled 2/3 c. raisins 1/2 c. onion, chopped 1 c. salad dressing Miracle Whip 1/2 c. sugar 2 Tbsp. vinegar 1/2 c. slivered almonds optional 1/2 c. sharp cheese optional Directions : Wash and cut broccoli into bite size pieces. Combine salad dressing, vinegar and sugar; mix well. Pour dressing mixture over other ingredients and toss. Refrigerate 2 hours before serving.

Fresh Coconut Cake

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1 c. butter 1 c. sugar 3 1/2 c. flour regular plain 3 1/2 tsp. baking powder 1 c. milk 8 egg whites, beaten 1/2 tsp. vanilla 1/2 tsp. lemon extract Directions : Cream butter; add sugar gradually, continuing to cream well. Combine flour and baking powder. Sift together and add to creamed mixture alternately with milk. Beat egg whites until stiff but not dry. Fold into creamed mixture.
