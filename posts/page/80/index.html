Duchess Potatoes

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 10 medium baking potatoes, peeled 1/4 c. butter or margarine 1/4 c. milk 3/4 tsp. salt 1/4 tsp. pepper 2 large eggs, lightly beaten 2 Tbsp. butter or margarine, melted paprika Directions : Cook potatoes in boiling water to cover 15 minutes or until tender. Drain and mash. Stir in butter and next 3 ingredients. Let cool 10 minutes. Stir in eggs. Spoon mixture into a large decorating bag fitted with a large star tip.

Dumbo S Double-Thick Chocolate Malted

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1/2 c. milk 2 scoops chocolate ice milk or ice cream 3 Tbsp. chocolate-flavored malted milk Directions : Pour the milk into a small mixing bowl. Add 1 scoop ice cream. Beat with an eggbeater until the ice cream is mixed in. Add the rest of the ice cream and the malted milk. Beat again until mixture is foamy. Pour into a glass to serve. Serves 1.
