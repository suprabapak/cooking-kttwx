Peanut Butter Rice Krispie Bars

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1 c. chunky peanut butter 1/2 c. sugar 1/2 c. white corn syrup Karo 2 c. Rice Krispies cereal 1 small pkg. chocolate chips Directions : Cream together peanut butter, sugar and corn syrup. Add Rice Krispies and put in an 8 x 8-inch pan. Press down.
