Crock-Pot Kielbasa

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 3 cans beer 1 small bottle ketchup 1 small jar mustard 1 tsp. vinegar 3/4 c. brown sugar 2 or 3 kielbasa Directions : Boil kielbasa in beer for 10 minutes. Combine ketchup, mustard, vinegar and sugar in crock-pot. Cut kielbasa in small bite size pieces and place in crock-pot. Cook on high for 2 to 3 hours or low for 3 to 5 hours. Enjoy.

Crunchy Onion Burger

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1 1/2 lb. ground beef 1 1/3 c. 2.8 oz. can French fried onions or fried onion rings 3/4 tsp. salt 1/4 tsp. salt 6 rolls Directions : Mix beef, 2/3 cup French fried onions and seasonings; shape into 6 burgers.
