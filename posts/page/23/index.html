Blueberry Heaven Supreme

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1 21 oz. can blueberry pie filling 1 8 1/4 oz. can crushed pineapple, drained 1/2 c. lemon juice 1 14 oz. can Eagle Brand milk 1 8 oz. container Cool Whip 2 graham cracker crusts miniature marshmallows Directions : Mix Eagle Brand milk and lemon juice until thickened. Fold in pie filling and drained pineapple. Fold in Cool Whip. Pour into pie shells. Garnish with graham cracker crumbs and/or miniature marshmallows.

Blueberry Muffins

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1 3/4 c. flour 1/3 c. sugar 2 1/2 tsp. baking powder 1/2 tsp. salt 1 c. blueberries 3/4 c. milk 1 egg 1/3 c. melted butter Directions : In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Stir in blueberries. Add milk, eggs and butter; mix just until dry ingredients are moistened. The batter will be lumpy. Do not overbeat. Spoon batter into 12 2 1/2-inch greased muffin cups.
