Pecan Pralines

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1 c. sugar 1 c. packed brown sugar 1/3 c. evaporated milk 1/2 stick butter or margarine, softened 1 1/2 c. pecans 1 1/2 c. coconut Directions : Combine sugars and evaporated milk in heavy saucepan. Cook over medium heat. Stir in butter, pecans and coconut. Cook until candy reaches soft ball stage. Mixture will form a soft ball when dropped in a cup of cold water.
