Buck Snort Slaw

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1 head cabbage 1 large onion 1 c. sugar 1 c. vinegar 1 tsp. celery seed 1/2 c. oil 2 tsp. salt Directions : Early in day 6 to 8 hours ahead, cut up cabbage and place in bowl. Slice onion and place on top of cabbage. Mix together and heat sugar, vinegar, celery seed, oil and salt. Heat until sugar dissolves. Pour over cabbage; do not stir.

Buckeye Candy

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1 box powdered sugar 8 oz. soft butter 1 8 oz. peanut butter paraffin 12 oz. chocolate chips Directions : Mix sugar, butter and peanut butter. Roll into balls and place on cookie sheet. Set in freezer for at least 30 minutes. Melt chocolate chips and paraffin in double boiler. Using a toothpick, dip balls 3/4 of way into chocolate chip and paraffin mixture to make them look like buckeyes.
