Lebanese Carrots

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 2 lb. carrots, cut on bias 1 medium onion, thinly sliced and separated 1 medium green pepper, thinly sliced 1 can tomato soup 3/4 c. sugar 1/3 c. vinegar 1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce 1 tsp. mustard Directions : Combine sauce ingredients. Add salt and pepper to taste. Pour over vegetables. Allow to sit refrigerated for 24 hours. Lemon Bars

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1 box lemon cake mix 1 stick butter or margarine, melted 1 egg 8 oz cream cheese 3 c. powdered sugar 2 eggs Directions : Blend cake mix, butter and egg together. Press into a 9 X 13 greased pan. Cream together the cream cheese and powdered sugar. Add eggs and mix until smooth. Pour over crust. Bake at 350 degrees for 35 to 40 minutes or until golden brown.
