Honey S Hominy Soup

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1 medium onion, chopped 1 green pepper, chopped 2 tsp. butter or oleo 1 can chicken broth 1 can hominy golden variety 1 can tomato soup 1 to 2 Tbsp. flour Directions : Saute peppers and onion in butter until translucent. Add 1 or 2 tablespoons of flour. Stir to mix well. Add chicken broth, stirring until mixture thickens. Add hominy and tomato soup. Mix thoroughly and continue heating to boiling point.
