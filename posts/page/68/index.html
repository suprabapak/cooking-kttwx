Cranberry Salad

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 2 3 oz. pkg. softened cream cheese 2 Tbsp. margarine 2 Tbsp. sugar 1 16 oz. can whole berry cranberry sauce 3/4 c. crushed pineapple, drained 1/2 c. chopped walnuts 1 c. whipping cream Directions : Mix cream cheese, margarine and sugar. Add fruit and walnuts. Whip cream and fold into fruit and cream cheese mixture. Pour into round mold. Freeze 6 hours. Remove from mold shortly before serving.
