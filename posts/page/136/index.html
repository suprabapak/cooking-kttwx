Microwave Zucchini-Beef Bake

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 3 Tbsp. olive oil 3 medium zucchini, sliced 1/2-inch thick 1 lb. ground sirloin 90% lean 1 small onion, chopped 1 garlic clove, minced 1 c. tomato sauce 1/2 tsp. salt 1/4 tsp. cinnamon 1/4 tsp. pepper 1/2 c. low-fat cottage cheese 1 egg 1/4 c. grated Parmesan cheese Directions : In a large skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat. Add zucchini slices and cook turning often until lightly browned, about 5 minutes.

Mildred Pardue S Blue Ribbon Carrot Cake

January 1, 0001

Millionaire Pie

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1 large container Cool Whip 1 large can crushed pineapple 1 can condensed milk 3 lemons 1 c. pecans 2 graham cracker crusts Directions : Empty Cool Whip into a bowl. Drain juice from pineapple. Mix Cool Whip and pineapple. Add condensed milk. Squeeze lemons, remove seeds and add to Cool Whip and pineapple. Chop nuts into small pieces and add to mixture. Stir all ingredients together and mix well.

Mississippi Mud Pie

January 1, 0001
