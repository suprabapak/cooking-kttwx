Dijon Chicken

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 6 whole chicken breasts, halved pepper 1 1/2 c. sour cream 1 8 oz. pkg. Italian breadcrumbs parsley salt garlic powder 1 1/2 c. Dijon mustard Directions : Lightly sprinkle chicken with salt, pepper and garlic powder. Combine sour cream and mustard in a shallow dish, mixing well. Dip each chicken breast into mustard mixture and dredge in breadcrumbs. Arrange chicken in single layer in baking pans.
