German Chocolate Upside-Down Cake

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1 c. pecans 1 c. coconut 1 box German chocolate cake mix 2 8 oz. pkg. cream cheese, softened 1 stick butter, softened 1 box powdered sugar Directions : Spread pecans and coconut on bottom of sheet cake pan. German Potato Salad

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 4 lb. potatoes, cooked 6 slices bacon, diced 1/2 c. sugar 3 Tbsp. flour 2 tsp. salt 1/4 tsp. pepper 1 c. cider vinegar 1 c. water 4 green onions, sliced Directions : Peel and cut potatoes in thin slices.
