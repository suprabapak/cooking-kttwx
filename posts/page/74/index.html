Cucumber Relish

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1 gal. cucumbers, peeled and cut 4 large onions 6 cloves garlic 4 bell peppers 1/3 c. salt 3 c. vinegar 4 1/2 c. sugar 1 tsp. turmeric Directions : Chop cucumbers, onions, garlic and peppers in blender. Cover with 1/3 cup salt in ice and let stand for 3 hours. Drain well. Mix vinegar, sugar and turmeric. Pour over cucumber mixture. Bring to a boil and simmer about 15 minutes.
