Central American Rice

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 2 Tbsp. oil 1 1/2 c. rice not instant 1 small onion, diced 1 medium tomato, diced 3 to 4 shakes garlic powder 3 chicken bouillon cubes 3 to 3 1/2 c. water Directions : Saute onion in oil. Add rice; stir constantly for 3 to 5 minutes. Add water, tomato, garlic powder and bouillon cubes that have been dissolved in the water. Reduce heat and cover.
