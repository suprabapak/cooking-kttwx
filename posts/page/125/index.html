Lemon Graham Cracker Pie

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1 12 oz. can evaporated milk, chilled 1 small pkg. lemon jello 1 c. boiling water Directions : Combine jello and water. Let cool but not set. Whip evaporated milk until thick in a large bowl. Add juice of 1 lemon. Beat in 3/4 cup of sugar. Add cooled Jell-O mix. Pour into 2 graham cracker shells. Sprinkle with graham cracker crumbs or sprinkles. Place in refrigerator until set.
