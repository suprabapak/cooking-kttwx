Hot Rolls

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1 c. sugar 1 c. lard 1 qt. sweet milk 1 yeast cake flour 1 tsp. baking powder 1 tsp. soda 2 tsp. salt Directions : Heat first 3 ingredients to boiling and cool to lukewarm. Add yeast and flour to make the consistency of cake dough. Set aside to rise for 2 hours. Add baking powder, soda and salt to the dough and more flour to make a stiff batter; let rise 2 hours before baking.

Hot SauceTo Can
