Cream Of Spinach Soup

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1 1/2 c. frozen leaf spinach, thawed and drained 1 1/4 c. chicken broth 3 Tbsp. margarine 1/4 c. chopped onion 2 Tbsp. flour 1/2 c. whipping cream dash of pepper dash of nutmeg Directions : Blend spinach and 1/2 cup chicken broth in blender until pureed. In saucepan, melt margarine. Add onion and saute until tender. Stir in flour and cook about 1 minute. Add remaining chicken broth and cook until thickened.
