Madge S Refrigerator Rolls

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1 Tbsp. sugar 1 pkg. yeast 1/2 c. lukewarm water 1 pt. milk 1/2 c. sugar 1/2 c. shortening or oleo 1 Tbsp. salt 2 egg whites, beaten stiff 6 c. sifted flour Directions : Put sugar over yeast in lukewarm water and let foam. Scald milk, sugar, oleo and salt. When cool, add dissolved yeast. Fold in egg whites, stir in flour. Dough should be soft.
