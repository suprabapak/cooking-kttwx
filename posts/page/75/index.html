Date Cake

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1 pkg. dates, cut fine 1 tsp. baking soda 1 c. hot water 1/2 c. margarine 1 c. sugar 1/2 tsp. salt 1/2 c. walnuts 1 egg 1 1/2 c. flour Directions : Sprinkle dates with baking soda and cover with hot water. Let stand until cool. Cream margarine, sugar and egg. Add dry ingredients, nuts and dates. Pour into lightly greased 8 x 8-inch pan and bake at 350u00b0 for at least 45 minutes.
