Foolproof Chocolate Fudge

January 1, 0001

Four-Inch Cheese Ball

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 2 c. shredded Cheddar cheese 3 Tbsp. salad dressing 1/2 tsp. Worcestershire sauce dash of onion, garlic and celery salts 1 3 oz. pkg. cream cheese 1/4 c. chopped ripe olives Directions : In a medium bowl, combine all ingredients, except olives. Mix well; stir in olives. Cover and chill until firm. Shape into a ball. Roll cheese ball in chopped nuts. Chill until firm. Serve with assorted crackers.
