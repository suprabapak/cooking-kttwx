Jim S Breakfast Delight

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1/4 c. flour 1/2 tsp. baking powder 1/4 tsp. salt 6 eggs, beaten 1 16 oz. carton small curd cottage cheese 1 14 oz. can corn, drained 8 oz. shredded Cheddar cheese green onion slices, pimentos and red pepper Directions : Stir together the dry ingredients. Add the remaining ingredients; mix well. Pour into greased 7 x 11-inch baking dish. Bake at 350u00b0 for 40 to 45 minutes, until set.

Johnny Menyetta

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1 lb. ground beef 1 green bell pepper 3 small green onions 1 stalk celery 1 pkg. wide noodles 1 can tomato soup 1/2 can water 1 c. grated Longhorn cheese 1 4 oz. can mushrooms Directions : Brown ground beef, bell pepper, onions and celery. Preboil noodles. Mix noodles with meat sauce. Add tomato soup, water and mushrooms. Cover with grated cheese. Bake at 350u00b0 for about 45 minutes; bake uncovered the last 15 minutes.

Judias EstofadasPinto Bean Soup

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1 lb. bag pinto beans 1 large onion, cut in half 3 large ripe tomatoes, peeled and halved 5 large cloves garlic 4 bay leaves 2 Tbsp. olive oil 2 ham hocks 2 Tbsp. paprika Directions : In a 5-quart pan, add 3 quarts of water and all ingredients, except garlic, olive oil and paprika. Mash garlic; mix with paprika and olive oil and add to the beans after beans are tender.

Judy S Vegetable Tortellini

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1 bag frozen chopped onions 1/2 tsp. minced garlic 2 Tbsp. oil 2 Tbsp. butter 1 lb. pkg. creamed spinach, softened 1/2 c. Parmesan cheese 1/2 c. Romano cheese 1 lb. box dry tortellini 1 pt. heavy cream Directions : Saute onions and garlic in butter and oil until transparent. Add spinach and heavy cream. Simmer; throw in cheeses. Stir and shut heat off. Make tortellini according to directions on box.

Kart-Wheels

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1 1/2 c. all-purpose flour 1/2 c. margarine or butter, softened 1/4 c. powdered sugar 1/2 tsp. baking powder 2 Tbsp. milk 1 c. pie filling or jam any flavor Directions : Heat oven to 375u00b0. Mix flour, margarine, powdered sugar, baking powder and just enough milk until dough forms. If dough seems dry, mix in more milk one teaspoon at a time. Divide dough into 6 equal parts.

Kate S Angel Food Cake

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1 1/2 c. egg whites 1/4 c. cold water 1 tsp. vanilla 1 1/2 tsp. cream of tartar 2 c. sugar 1 1/2 c. sifted cake flour Directions : Sift together three times the flour and 1 cup of sugar and set aside. Beat egg whites, water and vanilla until frothy, then add cream of tartar and continue beating until mixture stands in peaks but not dry.
