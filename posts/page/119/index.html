Jalapeno Cheese Squares

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 6 oz. grated Swiss cheese 6 oz. grated American cheese 6 oz. grated Cheddar cheese 6 beaten eggs 3 to 4 oz. can chopped jalapeno peppers or mild green chilies Directions : Preheat oven to 350u00b0. In greased 9 x 13-inch pan, place layer of cheeses and layer of peppers. Pour eggs over top. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes or until eggs are set. Cut into appetizer size squares.
