A & P Brownies

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 10 Tbsp. butter 2/3 c. cocoa 3 or 4 eggs 2 c. sugar 2 tsp. vanilla 1 c. flour 1/2 tsp. salt Directions : Melt butter on low heat. Add 2/3 cup cocoa. Stir until blended. Remove from heat. In a separate bowl, beat 3 or 4 eggs and sugar until lemon in color. Add to chocolate mixture. Stir in vanilla. Fold in flour and salt.

Aka Crispies

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1 box graham crackers 1/2 c. sugar 1 c. chopped pecans 1 stick butter 1 stick margarine 1/2 c. chopped almonds Directions : Line cookie sheet with foil. Place crackers as close as possible on sheet. Boil butter, margarine and sugar two minutes. Brush/pour over graham crackers and sprinkle with chopped nuts. Bake in preheated oven at 350u00b0 for 12 minutes. Cool and break into pieces.
