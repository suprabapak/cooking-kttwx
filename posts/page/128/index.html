M&M Cookies

January 1, 0001

Ingredients : 1 c. shortening 1 c. brown sugar 1/2 c. sugar 2 tsp. vanilla 1 1/2 c. M&Ms or chocolate chips 2 eggs 2 1/4 c. flour 1 tsp. baking soda 1 tsp. salt Directions : Blend shortening, brown and white sugar in bowl. Beat in vanilla and eggs. Sift remaining dry ingredients together, add to sugar, egg mixture blending well. Stir in M&Ms. Bake at 325u00b0 for 10 minutes.
